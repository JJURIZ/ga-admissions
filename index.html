Software Engineering problems provide me the most enjoyment, challenge, and gratification.

Assisting Others

Helping others is foundational to how I work.

Working with customers to make their lives easier provides a constant source of motivation.

Problem solving requires imagination.

Learning Continuously

I love to learn. It's one of the main reasons I chose software engineering for a career.

When I'm not coding I'm reading, listening to an audio book or podcast, or researching something.
